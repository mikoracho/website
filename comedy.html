Grown ups

After thirty years apart, five childhood best friends reunite in their hometown to attend the funeral of their beloved youth basketball coach. To celebrate his life and their 1978 championship win, the quintet rents a sprawling lake house for the Fourth of July weekend.

The Hangover

Two days before his wedding, Doug and his three best friends drive to Las Vegas for a legendary bachelor party they vow never to forget. The problem is, they wake up the next morning with no memory of the night before, a missing tooth, a tiger in the bathroom, and a six-month-old baby in the closet. Worst of all—the groom is nowhere to be found.

CATEGORY

Dumb and Dumber

Lloyd Christmas and Harry Dunne are two incredibly dim-witted but well-meaning best friends who find themselves in possession of a briefcase left behind by a beautiful woman at the airport. Convinced she’s in love with him, Lloyd persuades Harry to trade their jobs for a cross-country journey to Aspen, Colorado, to return the luggage.
