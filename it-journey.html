Essential Tools

As an IT student, I honestly can't imagine my daily tasks without tools like Canva, Google Workspace, and Virtual Machines. Canva helps me express ideas visually kahit wala akong strong design background, habang Google Docs at Drive keep my files organized and accessible kahit saan ako mag-login. Virtual Machines naman give me the freedom to experiment without fear of breaking my main system, which really helped me become more confident in exploring operating systems and software setups. These tools don't just make work easier‚Äîthey make learning more practical and less stressful for me.

From code suggestions to faster debugging, AI tools help me understand concepts na dati sobrang hirap intindihin. At the same time, may konting pressure din kasi mas mataas na ang expectations sa IT students and professionals.
