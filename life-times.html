Life and Times

Balancing student life, growth, and dreams

⏰ Daily Life • Miko Antonio S. Racho

As a 2nd year IT student, medyo busy talaga ang daily life ko pero natutunan ko na ring i balance ang school, rest, at personal time. Every day, I usually wake up early to prepare for my classes online man o face to face. Pagkatapos kumain, I check my schedule, deadlines, at mga activities lalo na sa programming at system related subjects. Hindi madali ang IT course kasi maraming problem solving at coding pero unti-unti akong nasasanay.

Time Management & Habits

One small habit that really improved my life is managing my time better. Dati, lagi akong nagpo-procrastinate, pero ngayon natutunan kong gawin agad ang tasks kahit paunti-unti. Kahit 30 minutes a day lang sa pag-aaral or practice ng coding, malaking tulong na. This habit helped me feel less stressed and more confident sa sarili ko. Naging malaking tulong din ang hobbies para maiwasan ang burnout sa school tulad ng:

  • Playing basketball
  • Watching movies
  • Going out with friends

What Keeps Me Going

What keeps me going during hard times is my dreams and the people who believe in me. Kapag nahihirapan ako sa subjects or feeling ko hindi ko kaya, iniisip ko kung bakit ako nagsimula. Gusto kong makapagtapos at magkaroon ng stable na future, hindi lang para sa sarili ko kundi para rin sa pamilya ko. I remind myself na lahat ng hirap ay temporary, pero ang success ay worth it.
