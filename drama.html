Titanic

Titanic is a sweeping romantic drama that unfolds against the backdrop of the ill-fated maiden voyage of the RMS Titanic. The film follows Jack and Rose, two passengers from vastly different social classes, whose passionate love story is tragically cut short by the ship's catastrophic sinking. With its blend of historical tragedy and timeless romance, Titanic explores themes of love, loss, and the human spirit's resilience in the face of disaster.

Good Will Hunting

Good Will Hunting is a heartfelt drama that follows the journey of Will Hunting, a janitor at MIT with a genius-level intellect. When his mathematical talents are discovered by a professor, Will is given the opportunity to turn his life around, but he must confront his troubled past and emotional barriers. With the help of a compassionate therapist, Will embarks on a path of self-discovery, exploring themes of identity, trauma, and the transformative power of human connection.
