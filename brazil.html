BRAZIL

Three-feature image layout

Brazil bursts with color, rhythm, and energy—from the Amazon’s vast wilderness to Rio’s golden beaches and iconic Christ the Redeemer. Its culture blends Afro-Brazilian roots, indigenous heritage, and European influences, creating vibrant festivals, music, and cuisine. Whether exploring colonial towns, lush rainforests, or modern cities, Brazil offers an unforgettable mix of adventure and warmth.
